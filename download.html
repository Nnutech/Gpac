Installation Guides

Follow these instructions to safely deploy GPac across your active environments.

01

Download the Installer

Click the direct download button for macOS in the platform grid to retrieve the official .dmg package.

02

Deploy to Applications

Double-click the downloaded .dmg file, then drag the GPac icon directly into your Applications folder.

03

Initialize and Trust

Open Launchpad, select GPac, and authenticate. If prompted by Gatekeeper, allow the launch in your System Settings under Privacy & Security.

01

Retrieve Installer

Select the Windows download link to save the installer executable (.exe) to your desktop directory.

02

Run Setup Wizard

Run the gpac-setup.exe file. Accept the terms and follow the system wizard to configure local file locations.

03

Configure Sync

Open the application, set up your desktop shortcut, and sign in to connect your active workspace databases.

01

Store Installation

Navigate directly to our official App Store product page to install GPac with a single tap, OR:

02

Manual Sideload

Download the signed .ipa package and deploy it via trusted sideloading tools like AltStore or Sideloadly using your computer.

03

Trust Profile

If sideloading, navigate to Settings > General > VPN & Device Management on your iOS device, and trust your Apple ID profile.

01

Store or Package

Search for GPac on the Google Play Store, or download the direct .apk payload file directly from our grid.

02

Enable Sideloading

If installing the raw APK, open your device's File Manager and permit the installation of applications from "unknown sources".

03

Finalize Permissions

Launch the package, grant local storage permissions if you intend to output PDF roadmaps, and initiate sync.

Need help setting up? Find direct answers to common deployment questions below.

help_center
Which grading systems does GPac support? expand_more

GPac is designed to support different university grading systems. Whether your institution uses a 4.0, 5.0, or a custom grading scale, you can configure the calculator to match your school's requirements.

Can I edit previous semesters later? expand_more

Yes. Your academic record isn't locked after you create it. You can revisit previous semesters, update grades or course information, and GPac will automatically recalculate your cumulative GPA.

Is GPac suitable for first-year students? expand_more

Absolutely. Whether you're calculating your first semester GPA or tracking your progress toward graduation, GPac is designed to grow with your academic journey.

Do I need an internet connection to use GPac? expand_more

Most of GPac's core features, including GPA calculation, prediction, and analytics, are designed to work locally on your device. Some future features may require an internet connection.
